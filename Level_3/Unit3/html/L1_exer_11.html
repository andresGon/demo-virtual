A: Hey Nicolas; did you hear about Stephen?

B: You mean Stephen who used to work here? What happened?

A: Well, I took my daughter into the hospital last week, and I saw Stephen working there.

B: He’s a doctor?

A: No, I don’t think so. I saw him carrying some files around, and he was wearing a suit and tie.

B: A suit and tie?

A: That’s right. He must be part of their admin team now.
