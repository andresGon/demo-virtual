conversation a

A: Hi Amy; how are things going with your homework?

B: Fine, thanks Mom. I’ve nearly finished it.

A: That’s good. You know, you haven’t mentioned Daniel for a long time. Isn’t he friends with you anymore?

B: Oh, we’re still friends. It’s just that he decided to leave uni at the start of the year.

A: Really? He left? His parents must be really upset with him!

B: Well, actually… He’s been developing a web-based company for the last few years, and it’s becoming really successful. So he wanted to concentrate on the company.

A: A web-based company? Can he really make money on the Internet?

B: He seems to be doing very well, and I think he finds it more relevant that all the theory we study at uni.

A: Well then, good luck to him!
