conversation b

A: Excuse me, Professor Brown.

B: Hello, Julia. Can I help you with something?

A: I was wondering if you knew anything about Fernanda. I’m supposed to work with her for the end-of-semester project, but I haven’t seen her recently.

B: Ah yes, Fernanda. I’m afraid that Fernanda had to leave the university. She wasn’t keeping up with her studies, and her attendance was terrible, so in the end, the university said she had to leave.

A: Oh dear, that’s terrible! I did think that she wasn’t taking the class seriously, but I thought that she could still pass it.

B: No, I’m afraid not. It seems that she had a few personal problems as well so she was quite distracted and couldn’t really focus on university.

A: I didn’t know that she had some problems. We talked a few times, but she never mentioned them.

B: Well, I’m sure she’ll be okay. I can’t really say anything else about it.

A: I understand. So what can I do about the end-of-semester project?

B: Let me have a think about it, and I’ll let you know.
