conversation b

M2: Hey Alberto, have you seen Miguel recently?

M3: Not for a few days. Why?

M2: A package for him was delivered to my house today.

M3: Oh… Well, you might need to keep that package for a while. I heard that Miguel and his wife broke up.

M2: Wow, really?

M3: That’s what I heard. And so Miguel has gone to live with his nephew in the city center.

M2: Do you know what happened with Miguel and his wife?

M3: To be honest, I heard some things, but I’m not supposed to tell anyone.

M2: Come on, you can give me a hint, can’t you?

M3: I’m sorry, but I can’t tell you.

M2: Well, I just hope they’re both okay…
