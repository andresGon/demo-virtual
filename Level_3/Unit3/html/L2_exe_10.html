F1: Really, Angela, you wouldn’t believe how much my life’s changed! Do you remember that I used to live in an apartment in the center of the city? Well, now I live with Juan in a house in the suburbs. It’s much quieter, and there are some good schools nearby, which will be great when he have kids.

F2: But isn’t it harder to get to work from there?

F1: Actually, it’s okay. I used to ride that old bike of mine, but now Juan and I have a car, so we drive to work together.

F2: That’s good. But how long does it take you to get home after work?

F1: Well, the traffic can be terrible! Sometimes we don’t get home until nearly eight o’clock. When I was living in the city, I used to go to the gym every evening, but now it’s impossible. We get home so late and so tired that we just have dinner, watch TV, and go to bed.

F2: Didn’t you use to watch TV before you were married?

F1: No, I used to hate it! But now I enjoy watching some programs with Juan.

F2: And do you save more money now?

F1: Yes, that’s one great thing. In the past, I didn’t use to save much at all, but now we save some money every month. We need to think about the future!
