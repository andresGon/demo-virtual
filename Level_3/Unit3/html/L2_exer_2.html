conversation a

M1: Jenny, what happened with you and David? You haven’t mentioned him for ages.

F1: Come on dad, I don’t want to talk about it.

M1: You know, if you don’t want to tell me, that’s okay, but…

F1: Well, if you have to know, we broke up. David was spending a lot of time with his cousin Alex, and I think Alex was a bad influence.

M1: How do you mean?

F1: He used to play a lot of sports, but a few weeks ago, I realized that David had started smoking. Alex smokes, so I think David picked up the habit from him.

M1: Really? He’s started smoking?

F1: That’s right, and I really didn’t like it. So that’s why I broke up with him.
