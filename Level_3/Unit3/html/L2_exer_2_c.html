F2: Judy, are you and the others going out this Friday?

F3: I guess so, we usually do! Why, do you want to go with us? Aren’t you going out with, um…

F2: Who, Tom? No, that’s all over now. We broke up a little while ago.

F3: Oh, I’m sorry to hear that. What happened?

F2: Nothing really happened, you know. It was just that we weren’t getting on well, and we realized we wanted different things from the relationship.

F3: It’s a pity when that happens. Didn’t he want to make a commitment to you?

F2: If you must know, it was the other way around. He wanted us to get married, but I didn’t want to.

F3: I guess that it’s difficult to give up your independence. Anyway, if you want to come out with us, that’d be great.

F2: I was wondering… (fade out)
