LESSON 3 > VOCABULARY 1 > WE CAN COMBINE THE VERB “GET”
WITH MANY DIFFERENT WORDS TO EXPRESS VARIOUS IDEAS.
For example:
Harry got drunk and then he got angry.
He was trying to get Penny back.
She tried to get away from the police.
Her agent says that Starly had got lost.
I hope he can get on with his acting career.
The meaning of the word “get” can have different meanings in this context. For example, it can mean “become”, “receive” or “go”.
