conversation c

A: Hi Tom, how are you?

B: Everything’s great, thanks Eddie. I haven’t seen you for ages!

A: Yes, it’s been a while, hasn’t it? How’s your new university going?

B: I’m really enjoying it. I’m much more interested in engineering than I was in law, and it’ll be great when I can start to build things.

A: Why did you start to study law if you didn’t like it so much?

B: It wasn’t really my choice, but my mom and dad thought that law was a better option for me. I said that I’d try it at least, but when the university gave us that huge list of texts to read, I had to face it that it wasn’t for me.

A: You’re right. There is a lot of reading. I can’t wait to finally finish!

B: I guess so. Still, you just have to keep working for the moment.
